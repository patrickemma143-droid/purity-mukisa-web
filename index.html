About Purity

A voice chosen
for such a time.

Born and raised in Uganda, Purity began her journey in worship through Scripture Union and church choirs as a teenager after giving her life to Christ.

What started as a voice surrendered to God has grown into a Spirit-led ministry touching hearts through worship, healing, and intimate encounters with God.

Beyond the music, Purity carries a deep passion for transforming lives through the Purity Charity — reaching vulnerable communities with hope, compassion, and purpose across Uganda.

More than a sound. A calling.
