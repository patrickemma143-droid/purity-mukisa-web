FAQ

Common Questions

How far in advance should I book Purity? +
We recommend booking at least 4–6 weeks in advance for local engagements, and 2–3 months for international events. However, we accommodate last-minute requests when the schedule allows.
Does Purity travel internationally? +
Yes. Purity has ministered in Kenya, Tanzania, Rwanda, South Africa, the United Kingdom, and the United States. International bookings require sufficient lead time and cover travel and accommodation.
What is included in a booking? +
A standard booking includes Purity and a backing track or acoustic accompaniment. Full band, choir, and production add-ons are available. All details are discussed and agreed upon in the booking contract.
Does Purity do free ministry events? +
Yes — community outreaches, school visits, and charity-focused events are considered case by case. Please reach out with details about your event and community.
How can I donate to the Purity Foundation? +
You can donate via MTN MoMo (060215), Airtel Money (1320567), Equity Bank (1025203511681), or FlexiPay (267071). Visit our Charity page for full details and the WhatsApp donation flow.
