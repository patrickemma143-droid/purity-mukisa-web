About Purity

A Voice Raised for
Such a Time as This

Purity is a gospel music artist, worship leader, and minister of the gospel based in Uganda. With a voice that carries both tenderness and authority, she has moved congregations across Africa and beyond into genuine encounters with God.

Her journey began in the choir at age 8. By 19 she had released her first album. Today, with 2 studio albums — Extravagant Love and Yenze — and concerts across 12 nations, Purity continues to walk in her calling — not as a performer, but as a vessel.

Beyond music, Purity leads the Purity Foundation — a charity dedicated to education, nutrition, and community development in underserved communities across Uganda. She believes the gospel must be heard AND felt.

Family Altar Meeting

FAM is a Spirit-led prayer and worship movement committed to restoring the altar of God in every home, family, and community across Uganda and beyond.

🔥

Weekly Prayer Gatherings

FAM hosts regular family altar meetings — online and in-person — where believers come together to worship, intercede, and hear the Word of God.

🏠

Restoring the Family Altar

At the heart of FAM is a passion to see every household become a house of prayer — where families worship together, pray together, and grow in faith together.

🌍

A Growing Community

FAM connects believers across Uganda and the diaspora in a community of accountability, love, and Spirit-filled worship. No one walks this journey alone.
